Sean Taylor - Award-Winning Video Game Producer

Hi, I'm a video game producer with twenty years experience of planning, designing and delivering interactive entertainment experiences across the web, mobile, television, console and social.

Currently, I am building the PlayHaus, a pop-up game entrepreneurship incubator, and finishing my first book, Punk Playthings: Provocations for 21st Century Game Making (CRC Press, Q4 2017).

I help good people turn ideas into interactive experiences:

  • I helped Denki make the BAFTA-winning word game, Quarrel.
  • I helped Sophia George, V&A Game Designer in Residence, make Strawberry Thief, an experimental plaything based on the work of William Morris.
  • I helped Mind Candy make the kids music streaming app, Moshi Music.
  • I helped Turbulenz deliver gorgeous, console-quality, HTML5 browser games.
  • I helped Reagent Games plan and prototype their amazing new plaything.
  • I helped EdTech startup, Systematic Learning produce a playable prototype of their pioneering EYFS playful phonics programme, Tap Tap Bat.
  • I helped DMA Design create the original Grand Theft Auto.

Let’s collaborate! I’m not doing too much consultancy these days but if you need a strategic thinker, project wrangler or mentor to help realise your vision of a more playful future, then we should talk.
